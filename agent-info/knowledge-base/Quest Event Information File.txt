ORAL ROBERTS UNIVERSITY - QUEST EVENT GUIDE & APPLICATION STEPS
================================================================

WHAT IS QUEST?
--------------
Quest is ORU's premier campus visitation event designed to give prospective students 
an immersive experience of life as a Golden Eagle. It is more than just a tour; 
it is an opportunity to connect with current students, professors, and leadership 
while experiencing the university's spiritual and academic culture.

KEY BENEFITS:
* Compete for the "Quest Whole Leadership Scholarship" (Full-Tuition).
* Experience ORU Chapel and worship services.
* Meet President Dr. William M. Wilson (Luncheon included).
* Tour the campus and residence halls.
* Attend academic breakout sessions specific to your major.
* Stay overnight in the dorms (Quest Plus only).

----------------------------------------------------------------
EVENT OPTIONS
----------------------------------------------------------------

1. QUEST (One-Day Experience)
   * Target Audience: High School Sophomores, Juniors, Seniors, and Transfer Students.
   * Cost: FREE.
   * Includes: Breakfast, campus tour, academic insights, Chapel, President's Luncheon, 
     and breakout sessions.
   * Best for: Students wanting a comprehensive overview without an overnight stay.

2. QUEST PLUS (Two-Day Experience)
   * Target Audience: High School Juniors, Seniors, and Transfer Students.
   * Cost: $50.
   * Includes: All "Quest" activities PLUS an overnight stay in the dorms, 
     class visits, dinner, evening activities (like basketball games or worship sets), 
     and breakfast the next morning.
   * Best for: Students wanting the full "day-in-the-life" experience.

3. ARTS QUEST (Specialty Event)
   * Focus: School of Worship, Media, & Performing Arts.
   * Target Audience: High School Juniors, Seniors, and Transfer Students.
   * Cost: $25.
   * Includes: Tour of the Media Arts Center, discipline-specific workshops, 
     auditions for talent scholarships, and free admission to an arts event.

STEPS TO APPLY / REGISTER FOR QUEST
----------------------------------------------------------------

STEP 1: VISIT THE REGISTRATION PAGE
   Go to the official ORU Quest website or use the direct portal link:
   https://oru.my.site.com/community/s/lt-event?id=a9ZUq0000009m8f

STEP 2: ACCESS THE GOLDEN EAGLE COMMUNITY PORTAL
   The registration link directs you to ORU's secure "Golden Eagle Community" site.
   * **Existing Users:** If you have applied to ORU before, log in with your 
     existing email and password.
   * **New Users:** If this is your first interaction, you MUST click "Sign Up" 
     or "Create an Account" to establish your visitor profile. You cannot register 
     as a guest without an account.

STEP 3: COMPLETE THE REGISTRATION FORM
   Once logged in, the specific Quest Event page will load.
   1. Verify your personal information is auto-filled correctly.
   2. Select your specific "Registrant Type" (Student vs. Parent).
   3. Choose your specific package (Quest vs. Quest Plus).
   4. **Quest Plus Note:** You will need to provide emergency contact info 
      and agree to the overnight housing waiver.

STEP 4: PAY THE FEE (If Applicable)
   * **Quest (Standard):** Free - Click "Submit" to finish.
   * **Quest Plus / Arts Quest:** You will be prompted to pay the $50 or $25 fee 
     via credit card within the portal to finalize your spot.

STEP 5: CONFIRMATION
   You will see a "Registration Confirmed" screen and receive an immediate email 
   from the Admissions team. This email contains your QR code for check-in 
   and a link to the event schedule app.

THE QUEST WHOLE LEADERSHIP SCHOLARSHIP
----------------------------------------------------------------
Attending Quest qualifies you to compete for a Full-Tuition Scholarship.

* Eligibility: High School Seniors and Transfer students accepted to ORU.
* Process:
  1. Register for a Quest event.
  2. You must be nominated (by a teacher, pastor, or counselor) or apply.
  3. Submit the "Quest Essay" prior to the event (deadlines apply).
  4. Participate in an interview during your Quest visit.
  
* Note: Always check the specific scholarship deadline dates on the website, 
  [cite_start]as they often require submission WEEKS before the actual event[cite: 2].
